Welcome

A Place to Belong,
A Purpose to Live

The Mapawa Baptist Youth Ministry exists to see young people encounter the living God, grow deep in His Word, and go boldly into the world as witnesses of the gospel.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a community to grow with, you are welcome here.

"Do not let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity."

— 1 Timothy 4:12

Our Vision
