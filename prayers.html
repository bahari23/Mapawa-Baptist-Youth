Why Prayer Matters

Prayer is not just asking God for things — it is entering into relationship with Him. When we pray, we acknowledge that He is God and we are not.

At Mapawa Baptist Youth Ministry, prayer is one of our four pillars. We pray in our gatherings, in our small groups, and for one another personally.
