Sermon Overview

This foundational message launches our youth ministry by answering three essential questions: What is the church? What is a youth ministry? And why does it matter for us today?

Drawing from Acts 2, we see the earliest blueprint of what Christian community looks like — not a programme, not a social club, but a Spirit-filled gathering of people transformed by the gospel and devoted to God and one another.

For young believers in Mapawa, this message is a call to take our faith seriously — to see the youth ministry not just as a weekend activity, but as a community where we meet God, are shaped by His Word, and are sent out as His witnesses.

Key Points

1. The Church is God's Idea

Before it was an institution, the church was a living family. God designed it — we didn't invent it, we were invited into it.

2. Youth Ministry is Discipleship in Action

We're not babysitting the next generation — we're raising disciples. Every gathering is an opportunity to know God deeper and follow Jesus closer.

3. You Are Here on Purpose

God has placed you in Mapawa Baptist at this time for a reason. You are not a spectator — you are a member of the body of Christ, needed and called.
