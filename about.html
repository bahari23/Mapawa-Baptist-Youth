About Our Ministry

Who We Are

Born Out of a Desire to See Youth Transformed

The Mapawa Baptist Church Youth Ministry is the youth wing of Mapawa Baptist Church — a congregation rooted in the Word of God, committed to prayer, and passionate about the next generation.

We gather young people aged 13–30 to worship, study the Scriptures, pray together, and go into the community with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our ministry is built on the conviction that every young person matters to God and that the church is uniquely called to disciple them into full devotion to Christ.

"Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it."

— Proverbs 22:6

Our Mission

To lead young people to saving faith in Jesus Christ, ground them in the Word, and equip them to glorify God in every aspect of life.

Our Vision

A generation of bold, grounded, grace-filled disciples who transform Mapawa and beyond for the glory of God.

Our Values

  • ✦ Scripture-centred teaching
  • ✦ Authentic community & fellowship
  • ✦ Prayer as a lifestyle
  • ✦ Service & outreach to the lost
  • ✦ Worship in spirit and truth
